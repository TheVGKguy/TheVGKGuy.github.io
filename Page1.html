Legoman's Origin

Legoman knew about Scratch when his science teacher recommended the site for Legoman's "growing mind." So, Legoman visited the site and joined Scratch with the username Linkinparklover2134. One month later, Legoman decided that Linkinparklover2134 was not a good username, so Legoman looked around for a different idea for a username. He set his eyes on "The Adventures of Legoman," a comic book Legoman was working on. At that moment, Legoman_Animator was born. A lot of Legoman's friends from Linkinparklover2134 thought that the username was better than the other username, so Legoman stuck with Legoman_Animator while deleting Linkinparklover2134.
